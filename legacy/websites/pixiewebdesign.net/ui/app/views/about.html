About pixiewebdesign.net

It all started about 23 years ago. I built a very simple C program, the purpose of which was to help primary school students to choose a post-primary (secondary) school. It hadn't been done before. At least not to my knowledge. I entered into the Aer Lingus Young Scientist exhibition, nowadays known as the BT Young Scientist. It was literally a series of if/else statements, and a flat-file lookup. But even at 14 years of age, I understood the impact that computers, and in particular software, would have on our daily lives.

And Im still building systems that help people with all sorts of life choices, today. Having worked with O2, Three and Trinity College during my 20 odd years programming and designing, I've learned a thing or two about how people interact with systems.
